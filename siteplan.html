Site Name: Youth and Family Hub

The site name ‘Youth and Family Hub’ was selected because it clearly reflects the organization’s mission to provide support, resources, and a safe space for both young people and their families.

Site Purpose:

The site provides a central hub for the Youth and Family Hub organization, offering information on programs and services for young people and their families. It will include resources for educational support, community events, counseling services, volunteer opportunities, and ways to get involved or donate to the organization.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A parent looking for after-school programs for their teenager visits the site to find information on available activities, schedules, and registration details.
  • Scenario 2: A young adult seeking counseling services navigates to the site to learn about the different types of support offered and how to book an appointment.
  • Scenario 3: A community member interested in volunteering checks the site for upcoming events and volunteer opportunities to get involved with the organization.
  • Scenario 4: A donor wants to contribute financially and visits the site to find donation options and understand how their contributions will be used to support youth and families.
